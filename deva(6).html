Famous Historical Places in India

Brihadeeswarar Temple, Thanjavur

A masterpiece of Chola architecture built in the 11th century and dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Taj Mahal, Agra

A symbol of love built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Qutub Minar, Delhi

The tallest brick minaret in the world, started in 1192 by Qutb-ud-din Aibak.
